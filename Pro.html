    Jagarnath puri

    Jagarnath Puri

  • Jagannath is a combination of two words, ‘Jagan’ meaning world and’ Nath’ meaning Lord. Thus Jagannath is the Lord of the Universe. Jagannath is the incarnation of Lord Vishnu or Lord Krishna. Jagannathis worshipped along with his brother Balarama and his sister Subhadra.

    Tirupati Balaji

    Tirupati Balaji

  • Tirupati Balaji temple is a very famous Hindu Vaishnavite Temple dedicated to Lord Venkateshwara, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu, situated in the hill town of Tirumal at Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh. It is believed that Lord Vishnu himself appeared here to save mankind in the troublesome Kaliyuga.

    • Kashi vishvanath

    Kashi Vishvanath

  • Located on the banks of the holy Ganges, Varanasi is regarded among the holiest of the Hindu cities. The Kashi Vishwanath temple is widely recognized as one of the most important places of worship in the Hindu religion. Inside the Kashi Vishwanath Temple is the Jyotirlinga of Shiva, Vishveshvara or Vishvanath. The Vishveshvara Jyotirlinga has a very special and unique significance in the spiritual history of India.
